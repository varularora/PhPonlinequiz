Playing with Mind

Online Quiz is a website that helps you to check how active is your brain in different languages such as Visual Basic, Java, C and C++.. You're free to take the quiz, before taking the quiz you have to register with us. The benefit of registration is that after taking the quiz you would be availabe to see your score in different fields.

Students benefit greatly from being able to take online practice quizzes. These quizzes focus on the critical lower-level thinking learning for the students. The students can practice these activities on online quizzes, therefore, freeing up class time for higher-level thinking activities. The benefits of taking online quiz are.

  • It gives you instant feedback, unlike paper examinations in a traditional classroom learning session
  • Students can retake a practice quiz as often as they want to improve their score.
  • It gives you the option of taking practice tests whenever you want
